Verizon Go90

For Verizon 90’s live music campaign, I designed storyboards and visual concepts that transformed the Verizon logo into a symphony of instruments—each one tuned to a different genre and audience. The creative challenge was to seamlessly blend branding with musical expression, reimagining the logo as turntables, drums,, and synths—each living in its own unique environment. Every frame had to feel distinct, like an album cover in motion. From ideation to 3D modeling, Guided by bold design choices, I iterated relentlessly to strike the perfect harmony between technology and sound.
