Miracle Ear: Hear a Better Day

As part of the 3D team for this Miracle-Ear campaign, I was responsible for modeling, animating, and texturing key moments that brought the experience of enhanced hearing to life. I created the whimsical reveal: lush trees unfurling, playful birds sweeping into frame, and vibrant environmental details emerging as sound awakens the world. I also textured the Miracle-Ear device for photoreal close-ups, ensuring it looked sleek and polished in every shot.
