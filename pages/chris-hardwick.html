Talking with Chris Hardwick

For AMC’s late-night talk show Talking with Chris Hardwick, I designed and animated the full graphics package, from show open to in-show elements. The creative revolved around the show’s bold logo, so I began by crafting a series of logo treatments to establish the visual tone—something loud, lively, and full of personality. Once I heard the music, it clicked: this needed to feel like a classic marquee bursting to life. I animated the logo with that in mind—bright, kinetic, and unapologetically showbiz—echoing the energy of celebrity conversations and late-night charm. A fast-talking, fast-moving identity built to light up the screen.
