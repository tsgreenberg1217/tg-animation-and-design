New York Mets on SNY

I designed and animated the broadcast package for the New York Mets on SNY—including the show open, pregame, and in-game graphics.

Channeling my knowledge of New York sports, I developed core visual motif of brick-building and rotunda columns to reflect the spirit of constructing Citi Field from the memories of the beloved Ebbets Field and The Polo Grounds. This foundation became the creative bedrock for the entire design system, with all future designs expanding on this vision.

I brought it all to life using Cinema 4D for 3D animation and After Effects for compositing. As someone whose first love was baseball, this project was a true home run ⚾
